About Me

I am a 22 year old Full Stack Developer who loves gaming, eating, music, movies, anime, and all things nerdy. For as long as I can remember technology has always played a significant role in my life. To be able to create lines of code on a project you're working on and watching it come to life as you constantly add more and more adjustments to it is amazing. Its almost mind blowing to me to even think about what you could create using your favorite framework and language. The possiblities are endless, as long as I have a creative mind I will continue polish my skills and code until I can't code anymore.

Project 1

For first my project I wanted to stand out when making this game, I didn't want it being just a simple jeopardy game with basic questions. So when making this I added my style to it, which is something I would want to see if i were playing a jeopardy game. Therefore I came up with the idea of making a Geek Jeopardy. The categories are Movies, Video games, Super Powers, Heroes/Villains, and Anime. Project 1.

Project 2

This project is titled "Kung-Fu Index". Over the years I have watched numerous martial arts movies involving big name actors such as Jackie Chan, Bruce Lee, Jet Li, and Donnie Yen. Since I am big into martial arts movies I developed an application that gives you the back story of the 4 actors I named. It goes into detail about the styles the actors use along with 4 clips from each of their own movies displaying their own unique styles of martial arts. Project 2

Project 3

For my third project I got inspired to do this app from watching the Fast and Furious movies and from playing the Need For Speed video games from my childhood. The purpose of this app is to let you choose from any 6 opponents to race against. You can log in and register or sign up and register your car, when you feel like racing you will be able to click on a racers car or the racer himself. If you want to see the racers car you can simply car click "Car Page" and it will display 6 vehicles, each with different stats. You can also click on the drivers to see their stats as well. Maybe you want to race against a rookie, or veteran, or an average oppenent. The choice is for you to decide who will be your rival. Project 3
